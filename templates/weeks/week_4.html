1. Fundamental Counting Principles

Product Rule (Multiplication)

If a task can be broken into a sequence of independent choices:

Total Ways = $n_1 \times n_2 \times ... \times n_k$

Example: Outfits. 3 shirts x 2 pants = 6 combinations.

Sum Rule (Addition)

If a task can be done in one of several mutually exclusive ways:

Total Ways = $n_1 + n_2 + ... + n_k$

Example: Picking a fruit. 3 apples OR 2 oranges = 5 choices.
